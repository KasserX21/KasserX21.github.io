Rev Runner

Momentum

  • Now only applies to solid blocks
  • Passive speed gain doubled
  • Max speed reduced from 43 m/s to 24 m/s

Laser Spear

  • New main weapon

All Rev'd Up

  • Cooldown: 18 seconds
  • Additional speed increased from 1 m/s to 10 m/s

Shockwave

  • No longer directly damages enemies in front
  • Creates an explosion behind you to propel forward
  • Immune to fall damage for 3 seconds
  • 0.8 second delay before losing all speed
Ever since 41–50, Rev Runner felt dated. This rework modernizes the kit, improves synergy, and adds clearer feedback. Laser Spear replaces a weapon that couldn’t keep up with extreme movement speed.

Pinja

  • Base size reduced by 15%

Star Collecting

  • Base size increased by 10% per level

Bat

  • Cooldown reduced from 1.8s to 1s
  • Reach increases by 25% per level
  • No longer stuns

Stun Bat

  • Cooldown increased to 2.4s
  • Stun damage conditions removed
  • Must fully charge to stun
  • Reach increases by 15% per level
  • Damage scales per level
  • Stun duration scales with Star Level

Bomb

  • New particle and sound cue 1 second before explosion
Pinja’s size and reach now better match the source. Bat and Stun Bat were split to reduce frustration and create clearer combat roles.

Elizabeth

MARVELOUS (New)

  • Resistance X for 1 tick
  • Tick-perfect parries grant bonuses and overhealth
  • Marvelous Parry resets MAGiC4LG1RL M3GA S7R1KE!

LETHAL;WEAPON

Elizabeth now leans harder into ranged combat. Battle No. 1 was replaced with a rhythm-based parry to better fit her identity and add skill expression.
